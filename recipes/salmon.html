Baked Salmon!

Ingredients

    Salmon Olive oil Fish rub One lemon Half a red onion Salt

Steps

    Preheat oven to 400F Lightly oil glass dish for oven Mix olive oil, salt, fish rub, and juice from half a lemon into a bowl Spread mixture on salmon, cover every bit Slice lemon and red onion, lay upon salmon Bake for 12 to 15 minutes, broil last two minutes if desired Enjoy a nice salmon!
