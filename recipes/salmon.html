Baked Salmon!

Ingredients

  • Salmon
  • Olive oli
  • Fish rub
  • One lemon
  • Half a red onion
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400F
  2. Lightly oli glass dish for oven
  3. Mix olive oli, salt, fish rub, and juice from half a lemon into a bowl
  4. Spread mixture on salmon, cover every bit
  5. Slice lemon and red onion, lay upon salmon
  6. Bake for 12 to 15 minutes, broli last two minutes if desired
  7. Enjoy a nice salmon!
