Tomato Soup

Tomato soup!

Ingredients

  • One can of 16.5 oz diced tomatoes
  • 46 oz tomato juice
  • 6 tbsp butter
  • One medium onion
  • Four to six tbsp of sugar
  • Two to three tbsp of chicken bullion (unsalted)
  • One fourth cup basil
  • One fourth cup Italian parsley
  • One and three fourths cup heavy cream
  • Black pepper and salt

Steps

  1. Cut up all ingredients before hand
  2. Let butter melt in a large pot on stove
  3. Add onion, let cook until onion is fragrent and translucent
  4. Add canned tomatoes, stir, then add tomato juice
  5. Before it gets too warm, add sugar, bullion and pepper (to taste)
  6. Just before it boils, turn off heat, add heavy cream and basil and parsley
  7. Enjoy!
